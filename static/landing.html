What Kroma does

Every tool you need to actually understand your documents, not just store them.

Document Chat
Ask anything. Get source-grounded answers from selected files, with citations and no internet search.
Flashcards
Turn any document into a deck of Q&A cards instantly. Flip to reveal, review at your own pace.
Quiz Mode
Test yourself with AI-generated multiple choice questions. Easy, Medium, or Hard. Scored with explanations.
Smart Summary
One click gets you a clean structured summary. Overview, key topics, and main takeaways ready in seconds.
Source Filtering
